How to Apply

Our admissions process is simple and straightforward

1

Initial Inquiry

Contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or visit our school to express your interest. Our admissions team will provide you with detailed information about available spaces and next steps.

2

School Tour & Assessment

Schedule a tour to see our facilities and meet our teachers. For primary classes, your child will have a brief, friendly assessment to help us understand their current level and learning needs.

3

Complete Registration

Submit required documents and complete the registration form. Pay the registration fee and first term fees to secure your child's place.

4

Welcome to Fahmid!

Receive your admission pack including uniforms information, school calendar, and parent handbook. Your child is now ready to begin their journey at Fahmid School!

Required Documents

Please prepare the following documents for registration

Birth Certificate

Original or certified copy of your child's birth certificate for age verification.

Passport Photos

Four recent passport-size photographs of your child on white background.

Medical Records

Immunization card and any relevant medical information or special needs documentation.

Previous School Records

Last report card and transfer certificate (if transferring from another school).

Parent/Guardian ID

Valid identification (National ID, Driver's License, or International Passport).

Proof of Address

Utility bill or any document showing your current residential address.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the academic year start?

The academic year typically begins in September and runs through July, divided into three terms with breaks for holidays.

Can I visit the school before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage all prospective parents to schedule a tour. Contact us to arrange a visit at a convenient time.

Do you accept mid-year admissions?

Yes, we accept students throughout the year subject to space availability. Contact us to check current openings.

What is your class size?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention. Classes typically have 15-20 students with dedicated teachers.

Do you provide transportation?

School bus services are available for select routes. Please inquire about availability and costs for your area.

How to Apply

Our Admissions Process

A straightforward process designed to make joining our school family as easy as possible.

1

Submit Enquiry

Complete the online enquiry form below or contact us directly by phone.

2

School Tour

Visit us to see our facilities and meet our staff in person.

3

Assessment

A brief, relaxed assessment appropriate to your child's age group.

4

Offer Letter

Receive a formal offer of placement if a space is available.

5

Enrol

Complete registration, submit documents, and pay the registration fee.

Entry Requirements

Year Groups & Entry Requirements

Nursery 1

Ages 2 – 3

  • Birth certificate
  • Immunisation record
  • Passport photograph (×4)
  • Parent/guardian ID
Nursery 2 & KG

Ages 3 – 5

  • All Nursery 1 documents
  • Previous school report (if any)
  • Informal readiness assessment
Primary 1 – 6

Ages 6 – 12

  • Birth certificate
  • Last two school reports
  • Passport photographs (×4)
  • Entry assessment (English & Maths)

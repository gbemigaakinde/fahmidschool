ℹ️ About Enrollment Period
  • Admission Term: First term the pupil started (fees begin from here)
  • Exit Term: Last term enrolled (fees stop after this)
  • Default: First Term to Third Term (full academic year)
  • Pupil is not billed for terms outside this period
Negative = discount · Positive = surcharge · Percentage applied first, then fixed amount
💰 How Fee Adjustment Works

Formula: Final Fee = (Base Fee × (1 + Percent/100)) + Amount

  • 50% scholarship: Percent = -50 → ₦20,000 × 0.5 = ₦10,000
  • ₦5,000 discount: Amount = -5000 → ₦20,000 − ₦5,000 = ₦15,000
  • Free: Percent = -100 → ₦0
  • Applies per term — leave blank for standard class fee

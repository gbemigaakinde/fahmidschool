Our Academic Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to build strong foundations and inspire lifelong learning

Academic Excellence at Fahmid

Our curriculum is carefully designed to provide age-appropriate learning experiences that develop intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for learning.

Balanced Curriculum

We combine academic rigor with creative expression, ensuring well-rounded development across all subject areas.

Small Class Sizes

Our low student-to-teacher ratio ensures personalized attention and support for every child's learning journey.

Proven Results

Our students consistently achieve excellent results and transition smoothly to secondary education with confidence.

Nursery Program (Ages 2-5)

Our nursery program provides a warm, nurturing environment where young children develop foundational skills through play-based learning.

Early Literacy

Introduction to letters, sounds, and simple words through songs, stories, and interactive activities.

Numeracy Skills

Learning numbers, counting, shapes, and basic mathematical concepts through hands-on exploration.

Creative Arts

Developing creativity and fine motor skills through art, music, and imaginative play activities.

Social Development

Building friendships, learning to share, and developing emotional intelligence in a supportive environment.

Primary Program (Primary 1-6)

Our primary curriculum builds strong academic foundations while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character development.

English Language

Reading comprehension, writing skills, grammar, vocabulary development, and oral communication through engaging literature and activities.

Mathematics

Number operations, problem-solving, mental mathematics, geometry, measurement, and practical application of mathematical concepts.

Science

Biology, chemistry, and physics concepts through hands-on experiments, observation, and exploration of the natural world.

Social Studies

History, geography, citizenship, and cultural awareness to develop informed, responsible global citizens.

Creative Arts

Visual arts, music, drama, and crafts to nurture creativity, self-expression, and artistic appreciation.

Physical Education

Sports, games, and physical activities promoting fitness, teamwork, and healthy lifestyle habits.

Assessment & Progress Tracking

We use comprehensive assessment methods to monitor each child's progress and provide regular feedback to parents.

Continuous Assessment

Regular classroom observations and evaluations to track daily learning progress and identify areas for support.

Termly Reports

Detailed report cards three times per year showing academic performance, behavior, and personal development.

Parent Meetings

Regular parent-teacher conferences to discuss progress, celebrate achievements, and address any concerns.

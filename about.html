About Fahmid School

Building tomorrow's leaders through excellence in education, character development, and a nurturing learning environment

Our Mission

To provide high-quality early childhood and primary education in a safe, caring environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child, preparing them for lifelong success.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution where children thrive academically, socially, and emotionally, becoming well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Fahmid School

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in teaching, learning, and all aspects of school life, inspiring students to reach their full potential.

Care & Respect

We create a nurturing environment where every child feels valued, safe, and respected for their unique qualities and contributions.

Integrity

We model and teach honesty, responsibility, and ethical behavior, building character alongside academic knowledge.

Community

We foster strong partnerships with parents and families, working together to support each child's growth and development.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methods and technology while maintaining proven educational practices that work.

Joy in Learning

We believe education should be engaging and enjoyable, sparking curiosity and a lifelong love of learning.

Our Story

Fahmid Nursery and Primary School was founded with a clear vision: to provide exceptional early childhood and primary education that prepares children for future success. From our beginnings as a small nursery, we have grown into a respected institution serving families throughout the community.

Our founders recognized the critical importance of the early years in a child's educational journey. They established a school where academic excellence is combined with character development, where each child is known and valued, and where learning is both rigorous and joyful.

Today, Fahmid School continues this legacy, serving hundreds of students and their families with dedication, expertise, and genuine care. Our graduates move on to secondary schools confident, curious, and well-prepared for the next stage of their educational journey.

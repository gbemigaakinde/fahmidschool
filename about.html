About Fahmid School

Our story, mission, values, and the people who make this school exceptional.

Our Mission

To be the first in providing learning and creativity in the hands of highly trained, loving, caring, and God-fearing teachers in a fun-filled environment.

Our Vision

To be the most referred home of learning celebrated for academic and moral excellence in Africa.

Our Story

From a Tutorial Centre to a Celebrated School

In 2006, the school began as a tutorial centre for secondary school students. At the time, the founder was working elsewhere but used every available opportunity to teach and support learners. After-hours and during short breaks, lessons were held, students were encouraged, and academic gaps were addressed.

What began as a small tutorial centre gradually grew. Students improved, parents noticed the results, and the centre became known as Multiple Success Tutorial. During holiday coaching periods, parents of younger children began to ask if their toddlers could also be taught there.

Running a full school was not the original plan. A turning point came when the founder was asked to head an existing school facing serious challenges. Despite efforts to rebuild trust, progress was limited — leading to a decisive moment.

In September 2007, a new school site was secured and operations began independently. That decision gave rise to Multiple Success Academy, which evolved into Fahmid Kiddies Academy and later into Fahmid Nursery and Primary School.

Through every stage of growth, one thing has remained unchanged: the commitment to raising well-rounded children — not only academically, but in character, discipline, confidence, and values.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles shape how we teach, how we treat our pupils, and how we run our school.

Love & Care

We show genuine love and care for every child, ensuring they feel safe, supported, and valued at all times.

Respect for the Individual

We respect each child as a unique individual and encourage confidence, self-worth, and personal growth.

Continuous Learning

We promote continuous learning and creativity, helping pupils think freely and develop lifelong learning skills.

Integrity

We uphold strong moral values and honesty, guiding pupils to do what is right in all circumstances.

Discipline

We instill discipline through positive guidance, helping pupils develop good behaviour and responsibility.

Teamwork

We encourage collaboration among pupils and staff, fostering cooperation, unity, and shared achievement.

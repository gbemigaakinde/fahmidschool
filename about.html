Our Story

In 2006, the school began as a tutorial centre for secondary school students. At the time, the founder was working elsewhere but used every available opportunity to teach and support learners. After work hours and even during short breaks, lessons were held, students were encouraged, and academic gaps were addressed. A manager was later employed to oversee daily activities, but the founder remained actively involved.

What began as a small tutorial centre gradually grew. Students showed improvement, parents noticed the results, and the centre became known as Multiple Success Tutorial. During holiday coaching periods, some parents of younger children began to show interest in the teaching methods and asked if their toddlers could also be taught there.

Running a full school was not the original plan. The focus then was simply on teaching and making a difference. However, a turning point came when the founder was asked to head an existing school that was facing serious challenges, including the withdrawal of many pupils. The responsibility was demanding, and despite efforts such as meeting parents personally and trying to rebuild trust, progress was limited.

This experience led to a crucial decision. Instead of abandoning the vision of quality education, a new path was chosen. In September 2007, a new school site was secured, and operations began independently.

That decision gave rise to Multiple Success Academy. As the years went by, the school evolved into Fahmid Kiddies Academy and later into Fahmid Nursery and Primary School. Through every stage of growth, one thing has remained unchanged: the commitment to raising well-rounded children. The school focuses not only on academics but also on character, discipline, confidence, and values. Our aim has always been to build a strong foundation for each child and to support their growth in all areas.

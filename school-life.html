School Life

Experience the engaging and supportive community that makes Fahmid special

A Day at Fahmid

Every day is filled with learning, discovery, faith, and growth

7:40 AM - 8:20 AM

Morning Assembly

The school day begins with assembly including prayers, national anthem, announcements, and a motivational message to start the day positively.

8:20 AM - 8:30 AM

Sports & Physical Activity

All pupils participate in short, energizing sports activities (held on Wednesdays and Fridays; other days may include light movement or readiness routines).

8:30 AM - 11:20 AM

Morning Academic Lessons

Focused classroom instruction in core subjects including English, mathematics, and foundational learning tailored to each class level.

11:20 AM - 11:50 AM

Long Break & Snack

Pupils enjoy a nutritious break, snacks, outdoor play, rest, and social time under supervision.

11:50 AM - 1:10 PM

Continuation of School Activities

More lessons, group work, practical activities, and subject continuation to build on morning learning.

1:10 PM - 2:00 PM

Solat (Prayer) Observation

Time allocated for Zuhr prayer and reflection that foster discipline, mindfulness, and faith-based values for Muslim pupils.

2:10 PM - 3:10 PM

Afternoon School Lessons

Final academic sessions of the day, including reinforcement of concepts, assessments, and interactive learning.

3:10 PM - 3:30 PM

Homework Assignment & Dismissal

Teachers assign and explain homework (3:10 PM – 3:25 PM). School closes at 3:30 PM with safe, organized dismissal.

Extracurricular Activities

Engaging programs that build confidence, communication, thinking skills and values

Sports & Physical Activities

Pupils enjoy fun physical activities and team games from time to time. Everyone looks forward to our annual Inter-House Sports event — a highlight of the year full of excitement, participation and school spirit.

Drama

Occasional drama activities give children the chance to act, express themselves, work together and build confidence in a joyful and supportive setting.

Sign Language

Regular weekly sessions teach basic sign language, helping pupils develop inclusive communication skills and greater awareness of others.

Coding

Weekly coding classes introduce logical thinking, problem-solving and basic programming in a fun, age-appropriate way.

Communication, Diction & French

Weekly dedicated sessions in Diction (clear speaking), Communication Skills (confident expression) and French help pupils speak more clearly, express ideas effectively and begin learning a second language.

Annual Events & Celebrations

Creating memorable experiences throughout the school year

Resumption Day

New academic session begins with welcome activities, class assignments, and orientation for new students and parents.

Independence Day

Celebrating Nigerian independence with cultural displays, traditional attire, patriotic songs, and learning about national heritage.

Children's Day

A special day dedicated to our students with games, entertainment, gifts, and fun activities celebrating childhood.

Cultural Day

Students showcase Nigeria's diverse cultures through traditional dress, food, dance, and presentations about different ethnic groups.

Sports Day

Annual athletics competition with races, relay events, and team sports. Houses compete for trophies and bragging rights!

Graduation Ceremony

Celebrating Primary 6 graduates as they complete their journey at Fahmid and prepare for secondary school.

Pupil Support & Wellbeing

We prioritize each child's emotional, social, and physical wellbeing

Pastoral Care

Dedicated staff provide emotional support, conflict resolution, and guidance to ensure every child feels safe and valued.

Health Services

Basic first aid available in the school with trained staff to handle minor injuries and health concerns during school hours.

Nutrition

Healthy meal options and snacks available. We promote good eating habits and ensure pupils have nutritious food.

Anti-Bullying

Zero-tolerance policy for bullying with clear procedures for reporting and addressing any concerns promptly and effectively.

Learning Support

Additional help for pupils who need extra support in specific subjects through remedial classes and one-on-one attention.

Safety & Security

Secure environment with controlled access, supervision during all activities, and emergency procedures to keep pupils safe.

Parent Partnership

We believe in strong collaboration between school and home

Regular Communication

Termly and spontaneous updates, progress reports, and open channels for parents to discuss their child's development with teachers.

Parent-Teacher Meetings

Termly conferences to discuss academic progress, behaviour, and ways parents can support learning at home.

Volunteer Opportunities

Parents are welcome to volunteer for school events, field trips, and special activities throughout the year.

Pupil/Parent Portal

Access grades, attendance records, school announcements, and communicate with teachers through our online portal.

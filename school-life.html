School Life

Experience the vibrant, engaging, and supportive community that makes Fahmid special

A Day at Fahmid

Every day is filled with learning, discovery, and fun

7:30 AM - 9:00 AM

Morning Assembly & Early Learning

Students arrive and gather for assembly with prayers, national anthem, announcements, and an inspiring message for the day. Early arrivals enjoy quiet reading or morning activities.

9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Core Academic Lessons

Focused instruction in English, Mathematics, and other core subjects. Teachers use interactive methods, group work, and hands-on activities to make learning engaging and effective.

11:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Break & Snack Time

Students enjoy nutritious snacks, outdoor play, and social time with friends. Supervised activities ensure safety while allowing children to recharge and have fun.

11:30 AM - 1:00 PM

Specialist Subjects & Activities

Time for Science, Social Studies, Arts, Music, or Physical Education. Students explore different subjects and develop well-rounded skills through varied activities.

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Review, Homework & Dismissal

Students review the day's lessons, receive homework assignments, and prepare to go home. Organized dismissal ensures each child leaves safely with their guardian or transport.

Extracurricular Activities

Beyond the classroom, students develop talents and interests through diverse activities

Sports & Athletics

Football, athletics, table tennis, and team games. Regular sports periods and annual sports day develop fitness, teamwork, and competitive spirit.

Music & Drama

Singing, dancing, and dramatic performances. Students participate in school concerts, cultural shows, and end-of-year productions.

Arts & Crafts

Drawing, painting, and creative projects. Art club allows students to express creativity and develop fine motor skills through various media.

Reading Club

Book discussions, library visits, and storytelling sessions. Fostering a love for reading and improving literacy through engaging activities.

Science Club

Hands-on experiments, nature walks, and science projects. Encouraging curiosity and exploration of the natural world through practical activities.

Leadership Programs

Prefect system, class monitors, and student council. Developing responsibility, leadership skills, and service to the school community.

Annual Events & Celebrations

Creating memorable experiences throughout the school year

Resumption Day

New academic session begins with welcome activities, class assignments, and orientation for new students and parents.

Independence Day

Celebrating Nigerian independence with cultural displays, traditional attire, patriotic songs, and learning about national heritage.

Children's Day

A special day dedicated to our students with games, entertainment, gifts, and fun activities celebrating childhood.

Cultural Day

Students showcase Nigeria's diverse cultures through traditional dress, food, dance, and presentations about different ethnic groups.

Sports Day

Annual athletics competition with races, relay events, and team sports. Houses compete for trophies and bragging rights!

Graduation Ceremony

Celebrating Primary 6 graduates as they complete their journey at Fahmid and prepare for secondary school.

Student Support & Wellbeing

We prioritize each child's emotional, social, and physical wellbeing

Pastoral Care

Dedicated staff provide emotional support, conflict resolution, and guidance to ensure every child feels safe and valued.

Health Services

Basic first aid available on campus with trained staff to handle minor injuries and health concerns during school hours.

Nutrition

Healthy meal options and snacks available. We promote good eating habits and ensure students have nutritious food.

Anti-Bullying

Zero-tolerance policy for bullying with clear procedures for reporting and addressing any concerns promptly and effectively.

Learning Support

Additional help for students who need extra support in specific subjects through remedial classes and one-on-one attention.

Safety & Security

Secure campus with controlled access, supervision during all activities, and emergency procedures to keep students safe.

Parent Partnership

We believe in strong collaboration between school and home

Regular Communication

Weekly newsletters, progress reports, and open channels for parents to discuss their child's development with teachers.

Parent-Teacher Meetings

Termly conferences to discuss academic progress, behavior, and ways parents can support learning at home.

Volunteer Opportunities

Parents are welcome to volunteer for school events, field trips, and special activities throughout the year.

Parent Portal

Access grades, attendance records, school announcements, and communicate with teachers through our online portal.

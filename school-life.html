School Life

Experience the engaging, supportive, and faith-centred community that makes Fahmid a place where children truly thrive.

Daily Rhythm

A Day at Fahmid

Every school day follows a structured, balanced routine that weaves together academic learning, physical activity, spiritual practice, and social development.

This consistent rhythm gives pupils a sense of security and purpose, helping them get the very best out of each day.

School Hours

Gates open 7:30 AM
Assembly begins 7:40 AM
Long break 11:20 – 11:50 AM
School closes 3:30 PM
7:40 AM – 8:20 AM
Morning Assembly

The day begins with prayers, the national anthem, school announcements, and a motivational message to set a positive tone.

8:20 AM – 8:30 AM
Sports & Physical Activity

Energising physical activities for all pupils, with full sports sessions on Wednesdays and Fridays, and light movement routines on other days.

8:30 AM – 11:20 AM
Morning Academic Lessons

Focused classroom instruction in core subjects — English, Mathematics, and foundational learning tailored to each class level.

11:20 AM – 11:50 AM
Long Break & Snack

Pupils enjoy nutritious snacks, outdoor play, rest, and social time in a supervised environment.

11:50 AM – 1:10 PM
Continuation of School Activities

More lessons, group work, practical activities, and subject continuation to build on morning learning.

1:10 PM – 2:00 PM
Ṣalāt (Prayer) Observation

Dedicated time for Zuhr prayer and quiet reflection, fostering discipline, mindfulness, and faith-based values.

2:10 PM – 3:10 PM
Afternoon Lessons

Final academic sessions of the day, including concept reinforcement, assessments, and interactive learning activities.

3:10 PM – 3:30 PM
Homework & Dismissal

Teachers assign and explain homework. School closes at 3:30 PM with safe, organised dismissal for all pupils.

Beyond the Classroom

Extracurricular Activities

Enriching programmes that develop confidence, communication, creativity, and lifelong values in every child.

Sports & Physical Activities

Fun physical activities and team games throughout the year, with our annual Inter-House Sports Day as the most-anticipated highlight — full of competition, school spirit, and celebration.

Drama

Occasional drama sessions give children the chance to act, express themselves, and collaborate in a joyful and supportive setting — building confidence and creativity.

Sign Language

Weekly sessions teach basic sign language, helping pupils develop inclusive communication skills and a greater awareness and respect for others.

Coding

Weekly coding classes introduce logical thinking, problem-solving, and basic programming concepts in a fun and age-appropriate way that prepares pupils for a digital future.

Communication, Diction & French

Weekly dedicated sessions in Diction (clear speaking), Communication Skills (confident, expressive delivery), and French help pupils articulate ideas clearly, express themselves effectively, and take their first steps in learning a second language — preparing them for a world beyond school.

Marking the Year

Annual Events & Celebrations

Memorable moments that bring our whole school community together throughout the year.

Resumption Day

New term begins with welcome activities, class assignments, and orientation for new pupils and their families.

Independence Day

Celebrating Nigerian independence with cultural displays, traditional attire, patriotic songs, and lessons on national heritage.

Children's Day

A special day dedicated entirely to our pupils — games, entertainment, gifts, and fun activities that celebrate the joy of childhood.

Cultural Day

Pupils showcase Nigeria's rich diversity through traditional dress, food, dance, and presentations about different ethnic groups and traditions.

Inter-House Sports Day

Annual athletics competition with races, relay events, and team sports. Houses compete fiercely for trophies in a day full of school spirit.

Graduation Ceremony

A proud celebration as Primary 6 pupils complete their Fahmid journey and prepare to embark on the next chapter at secondary school.

Holistic Wellbeing

Pupil Support & Wellbeing

We prioritise each child's emotional, social, and physical wellbeing — because happy children learn better.

Pastoral Care

Dedicated staff provide emotional support, conflict resolution, and personal guidance so every child feels safe, seen, and valued within our school community.

Health Services

Basic first aid is available at all times, with trained staff on hand to handle minor injuries and health concerns during school hours.

Nutrition

Healthy meal options and snacks are available. We promote good eating habits and ensure every pupil has access to nutritious food each day.

Anti-Bullying

A zero-tolerance policy for bullying, with clear and transparent procedures for reporting and addressing any concerns promptly and effectively.

Learning Support

Additional help for pupils who need extra support through remedial classes, targeted revision, and one-on-one teacher attention outside of regular lessons.

Safety & Security

A secure campus with controlled access, active supervision during all activities, and clear emergency procedures to keep every pupil safe at all times.

Working Together

Parent Partnership

We believe that children thrive when school and home work closely together. That's why we maintain open, regular, and meaningful communication with every family.

Get in Touch Parent Portal

Regular Communication

Termly progress reports and open channels for parents to discuss their child's development at any time.

Parent-Teacher Meetings

Termly conferences to discuss academic progress, behaviour, and how parents can best support learning at home.

Volunteer Opportunities

Parents are warmly welcome to volunteer for school events, field trips, and special activities throughout the year.

Parent & Pupil Portal

Access grades, attendance, announcements, and communicate directly with teachers through our secure online portal.
